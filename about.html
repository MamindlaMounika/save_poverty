About Us

Helping Hands is a non-profit initiative that provides a platform for individuals living below the poverty line to showcase their services and products. Our mission is to empower these individuals by connecting them to a broader audience and helping them earn a sustainable income.

We believe in the power of community and small actions to make a big difference. By supporting this initiative, you are directly contributing to uplifting lives, one step at a time.

Thank you for joining hands with us to create a better future for everyone.
